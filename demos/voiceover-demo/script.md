## intro
Have you ever wondered, [[cue:question]]when we talk to an AI, how does it make sense of what we say?

## token-1
It does not read language the way we do. [[cue:reveal]]It reads tokens.

## token-2
A token is one of the smallest units of information an AI processes.
For example, the phrase "artificial intelligence" [[cue:split]]can be handled as separate pieces: artificial, intelligence.

## ending
So the next time you hear "a one-million-token context window," [[cue:context]]you will know it describes how many pieces like these the AI can keep in view at once.
